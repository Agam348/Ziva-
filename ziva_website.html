Our Story

Where Brilliance
Meets the Plate

Ziva — meaning brilliance and brightness — is Amritsar's celebrated all-day dining destination, nestled inside the Holiday Inn on Ranjit Avenue. We bring together the finest flavours of North India, global cuisines, and artisanal desserts under one roof, crafted with a commitment to quality that has earned us recognition across the city.

From sunrise breakfasts to candlelit dinners, every moment at Ziva is designed to be memorable. Our culinary team sources the freshest ingredients, balanced with bold flavours and presented with the elegance you deserve.
